If you are filtering by:
  • ID: Specify the ID number of the item you are looking for. ("6")
  • Title: Input a specific title or fragments of a title. ("Moana" or "M")
  • Director: Input a specific title or fragments of a title. ("Spielberg" or "Spiel")
  • Rating: For shows, the rating must start with "TV-" ("TV-Y", "TV-G", "TV-PG", "TV-Y7", "TV-14", "TV-MA", etc.). For movies, they are in the standard format. ("G", "PG", "PG-13", "R", etc.)
  • Cast: Input a specific name of an actor or fragments of a name. ("Adam Sandler" or "Adam")
  • Country: Specify the country by inputting the entire name spelled out fully with no abbreviations. You may also search by fragments of a name. ("United States" or "United", no abbreviations like "US" or "USA")
  • Release Year: Input the specific year you are looking for. ("2007")
  • Genre: Specify the genre you are looking for, spelling the genre name out fully, or input a fragment of a genre. ("Documentaries" or "Docu") If you are looking for a TV show genre, include "TV" at the beginning. ("TV Dramas")
    • ** For all text inputs, you can filter by inputting the entire string or inputting a fragment of a string.
